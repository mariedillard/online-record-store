About Us

A punk record store located in a dimly lit alley in Seattle, WA. We don't just sell punk. Our small store contains cd's, vinyl, and cassetes of any genre your cold little heart could crave.

Named after the noble russian sighthound, you might see a few of our borzois wandering the aisles. Feel free to give them a pat on the head if they let you. The owner owns five.
